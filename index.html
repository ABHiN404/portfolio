The Argentina national football team, popularly known as La Albiceleste ("The White and Sky Blue"), represents Argentina in international men's football. Managed by the Argentine Football Association, the team is one of the most successful and respected national sides in football history.

  • World Cup Titles: 3 (1978, 1986, 2022)
  • Copa América titles: 16 (Record holder as of 2024)
  • Current captain: Lionel Messi
  • Home stadium: Estadio Monumental
  • Head coach: Lionel Scaloni
